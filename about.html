About This Lab Environment

A structured enterprise networking and security simulation environment aligned with CCNP-level concepts and SOC operational workflows.

This environment is an evolving technical lab designed to bridge networking, security, and infrastructure concepts through hands-on implementation. It focuses on applying theoretical knowledge in a controlled, repeatable setup rather than static documentation.

The system is structured in progressive phases covering enterprise networking fundamentals, routing and services expansion, and finally security operations with monitoring and automation integration. Each layer builds toward a production-like enterprise simulation model.

The goal is to develop operational capability in designing, deploying, and troubleshooting enterprise systems while integrating visibility and automation workflows commonly found in modern infrastructure environments.

Portfolio Summary

This portfolio demonstrates practical experience in infrastructure design, dynamic routing, and SOC operations through multi-phase deployments across networking and security domains, supported by detailed technical documentation.

It also includes additional projects, study materials, and cybersecurity practical walkthroughs (TryHackMe platform) archived throughout the learning and project progression.
